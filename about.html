About

I trace artifacts—books, manuscripts, objects with gaps in their history or questions hanging over their authenticity. It's detective work, really, just with older clues and dustier evidence.

The work takes me through archives (national libraries, municipal records, auction house databases), and sometimes means getting on a plane when the trail leads somewhere that requires showing up in person. A lead in Vienna, a dealer in Istanbul, an estate sale outside Edinburgh. Most investigations wrap up in a few weeks, though some have stayed open for months, waiting for the right piece of information to surface.

I'm based in the UK, work globally, and prefer email for initial contact. If you've got something that needs investigating, send over photos and whatever documentation you have—provenance records, receipts, family stories, anything that adds context. I'll take a look and let you know what's possible.
