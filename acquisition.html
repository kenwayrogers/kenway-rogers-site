Services Offered

My Network

Ethical Standards

I operate under strict ethical guidelines:

  • No Looting: I will not acquire artifacts obtained through illegal excavation or theft
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Respect for source communities and cultural patrimony laws
  • Transparency: Full disclosure of an artifact's history and any gaps in provenance
  • Legal Compliance: Adherence to UNESCO conventions and national heritage laws
  • Documentation: Comprehensive records for every transaction

  • Investors interested in tangible cultural assets
