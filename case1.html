The Forgotten Compass

A case study on tracing an instrument through maritime records and hidden makings.

Overview

This study documents the discovery of a brass field compass with atypical etchings; the investigation combined fieldwork, carbon dating, and local archives to trace its origins.

Findings

  • Compass shows markers consistent with early storm-sighting ellipses.
  • Etchings match a cartographer's cipher used around 1820 in the South Sea trade.
  • Minor restoration recommended to prevent finish degradation.
