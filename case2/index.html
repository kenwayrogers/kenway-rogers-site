The Emerald Ledger

A coded ledger with multiple entries and a secret map hidden among ledger notations.

Overview

Recovered from a street market, this ledger held a set of notations that upon closer examination made use of an uncommon cipher and referenced a shipment manifest.

Methodology

  • Transcribed ledger entries and cross-referenced manifest dates.
  • Decoded cipher through frequency analysis and matched to a regional dialect.
  • Found a map overlay indicating an unregistered storage site.
