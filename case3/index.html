Steel of the Highlands

A study on the origins and craftsmanship of a ceremonial blade recovered from ruins.

Overview

This article traces the forging techniques, stylistic motifs, and cultural use surrounding a highland blade removed from a collapsed temple site.

Findings

  • Blade composition consistent with a wootz-style carbon signature.
  • Inscription fragments reference a lineage and ritual use.
  • Restoration avoided to maintain original patina, while recommended stasis measures were applied.
